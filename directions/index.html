Travel Information

Tianjin is the fourth largest city in China, located 120 km southeast of Beijing, metropolitan population 9-10 million. Tianjin is one of the commercial and industrial centers in mideast-northeast of China. It is also one of the educational centers in China and is the home of the two oldest and well-known universities in China â€” Nankai University and Tianjin University. In recent years, Tianjin has taken great stride to modernize the city, including its commerce and industries. It is re-emerging as one of the dynamic and bustling metropolitan cities in China.

Travel Arrangements

1. For visitors arriving at Beijing Capital International Airport, please take airport shuttle bus (board at Gate 1 on the first floor of Terminal 3 or Gate 15 on the first floor of Terminal 2) to Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station (Airport shuttle bus runs from 6:30 to 23:00; long-distance bus runs from 5:00 to 18:00 and charges 82 rmb for each person. Departure Timetable), then take a taxi to Nankai University or follow the direction No.5. There is another way if you prefer to take the high-speed rail: after arriving at Beijing Capital International Airport, please take airport shuttle bus or metro to Beijingnan Railway Station, then transfer to Tianjin Railway Station, Tianjinnan Railway Station or Tianjinxi Railway Station by high-speed rail. From Tianjin railway stations to Nankai University, please see the following directions No.3, No.4 and No.5.

2. For visitors arriving at Tianjin Binhai International Airport, please take metro line 2 (bound for Caozhuang Station) to Tianjin Railway Station, then change to metro line 3 (bound for Tianjinnan Railway Station), get off at Tianta Station and walk to Nankai University.

3. For visitors arriving at Tianjin Railway Station, please take metro line 3 (bound for Tianjinnan Railway Station) to Tianta Station and walk to Nankai University, or take bus No. 8/No. 832 and get off at Balitai Station (near the north gate of Nankai University), or take bus No. 845 and get off at Shoubiaochang Station (near the southwest gate of Nankai University).

4. For visitors arriving at Tianjinnan Railway Station, please take metro line 3 (bound for Xiaodian Station) to Tianta Station and walk to Nankai University.

5. For visitors arriving at Tianjinxi Railway Station, please take metro line 1 (bound for Shuanglin Station) to Yingkoudao Station, then change to metro line 3 (bound for Tianjinnan Railway Station), get off at Tianta Station and walk to Nankai University; or take bus No. 859 at the South Square of Tianjinxi Railway Station and get off at Balitai Station (near the north gate of Nankai University).
